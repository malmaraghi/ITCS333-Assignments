Welcome Message

We are excited to welcome you to Sunshine Kindergarten, located in Manama the heart of Bahrain! Here, we believe in fostering creativity, exploration, and growth through play and learning. Our kindergarten is designed to help your child develop important social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a safe and nurturing environment.

Ms. Eman, our teacher at Sunshine Kindergarten, has over 10 years of experience in early childhood education. She holds a degree in Early Childhood Development and is trained in child psychology and creative teaching. Ms. Eman fosters hands-on learning and is dedicated to helping your child grow and explore.
